Our Story

Started with a Passion for Teaching

NextGen Academy was founded over ten years ago with one mission: make quality education accessible to every student, regardless of their background or location. What started as a small tuition centre in Karachi has grown into a full academic institution.

We have proudly taught students from some of Karachi's most reputed institutions — Happy Home, Al Murtaza, Fatimiyah, and Beaconhouse — and our reach has expanded internationally, with online sessions for students in the UK, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.

Today we offer tuitions for Classes 9–12 across Science, Commerce, and Accounts streams, alongside a Summer Vacation Programme and dedicated career counselling.
